## Who Is CybroHost?

CybroHost is a hosting provider based out of Bulgaria, running their dedicated infrastructure out of the S3C Data Center in Sofia. The thing that stands out early on: their pricing is transparent. No surprise renewal spikes, no hidden add-ons for SSL. What you see is what you pay.
